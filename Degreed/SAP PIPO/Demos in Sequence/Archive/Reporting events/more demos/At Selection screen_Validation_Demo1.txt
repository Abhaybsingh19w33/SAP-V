PARAMETERS: CITY_FR(20), CITY_TO(20).

AT SELECTION-SCREEN ON CITY_FR.
  IF CITY_FR NE 'MUMBAI'.
    MESSAGE E001(Z104329MSGCLAS) WITH 'City from must be Mumbai'.
  ENDIF.

AT SELECTION-SCREEN ON CITY_TO.
  IF CITY_TO NE 'CHENNAI'.
    MESSAGE E001(Z104329MSGCLAS) WITH 'City to must be Chennai'.
  ENDIF.

START-OF-SELECTION.
  WRITE CITY_FR.
  WRITE CITY_TO.
