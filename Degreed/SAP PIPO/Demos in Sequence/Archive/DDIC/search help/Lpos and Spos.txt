LPOS -> Position of field in search help results report/table. 
From here you select the value

SPOS->  Position of the search help parameter in the dialog box 
for limiting the hit list.

Here is the example to make more clear.

A. matnr      spos = 2  , lpos = 1 
B. maktx      spos = 1  , lpos = 3 
C. vbeln      spos = 3  , lpos = 2


then when you press F4 ... the selection screen will be displayed with 
SPOS

1.MAKTX
2.MATNR
3.VBELN


When press enter you will see the data displayed for above fields as 
LPOS

1.MATNR
2.VBELN
3.MATKX
